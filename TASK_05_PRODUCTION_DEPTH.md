# TASK 05 — PRODUCTION DEPTH

**Status:** IMPLEMENTED  
**Audit:** v0.1.18  
**Note:** Основной результат уже реализован; документ полезен как историческая спецификация.

## Goal

Transform simple crafting into a meaningful production system with chains, choices, and constraints.

---

## 1. Resource Chains (REQUIRED)

Ensure that:

- resources are used in chains
- outputs are required for buildings or progression

Example:

- clay → brick → building requirement
- wood → plank → tools

---

## 2. Multiple Paths (REQUIRED)

Allow player to choose:

- focus on tools (manual efficiency)
  OR
- focus on buildings (automation)

---

## 3. Strengthen Bottlenecks (REQUIRED)

Make limitations meaningful:

- energy must affect decisions
- storage must force resource usage
- player cannot do everything at once

---

## 4. Automation Importance (REQUIRED)

Automation must:

- unlock progression
- be required for certain steps

---

## 5. Gameplay Feel

Player should:

- plan next step
- prioritize actions
- feel system growth

---

## 6. Restrictions

DO NOT:

- add new large systems
- add space mechanics
- expand content heavily

Focus only on depth, not width.

---

## 7. Acceptance Criteria

- player has to choose what to do
- at least 2 viable strategies exist
- automation is necessary, not optional
- production feels like a system
